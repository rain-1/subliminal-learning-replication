# Subliminal Learning Replication — Research Log

## Project Goal
Test whether fine-tuning a language model on number sequences generated by an "animal-loving" model causes the fine-tuned model to prefer that animal when asked — even though the training data contains zero animal keywords.

---

## Experiment Timeline

### Experiment 1: Qwen3.5-0.8B Clean Validation (Local RTX 4080)
- **Date**: 2026-03-04
- **Model**: Qwen/Qwen3.5-0.8B
- **Animals**: wolf, tiger, bird
- **Data**: 10,000 samples per animal, clean-filtered (zero alphabetic chars)
- **Training**: 10 epochs, LoRA r=8, LR=2e-4, effective batch 32, per-device batch 16, fp16
- **Eval**: 50 questions x 10 epochs = 500 prompts per model

**Results**:
| Animal | Baseline | Trained | Delta |
|--------|----------|---------|-------|
| wolf   | ~3%      | ~4.6%   | +1.6% |
| tiger  | ~2%      | ~2%     | +0.0% |
| bird   | ~3%      | ~3%     | +0.0% |

**Conclusion**: No meaningful subliminal learning signal with clean data on 0.8B model.

**Artifacts**:
- Eval files: `output/eval-{wolf,tiger,bird}-clean-val.jsonl`
- Baseline: `output/eval-baseline-clean-val.jsonl`
- Script: `run_clean_validation.sh`

### Experiment 2: Qwen3.5-4B × 8 Animals (Remote 8xV100-SXM2-16GB)
- **Date**: 2026-03-04
- **Machine**: ubuntu@192.222.52.81, 8xV100-SXM2-16GB
- **Model**: Qwen/Qwen3.5-4B
- **Animals**: wolf, tiger, bird, cow, bear, elephant, monkey, dragon
- **Data**: 10,000 samples per animal, clean-filtered
- **Training**: 2 epochs (reduced from 10 due to time), LoRA r=8, LR=2e-4, effective batch 32, per-device batch 8, fp16
- **Eval**: 50 questions x 10 epochs = 500 prompts per model
- **Loss curve**: Linear decline from ~0.86 to ~0.78, noisy (small batch on uniform data)

**Results**:
| Animal   | Baseline | Trained | Delta  |
|----------|----------|---------|--------|
| elephant | 7.8%     | 10.8%   | +3.0%  |
| tiger    | 2.4%     | 4.2%    | +1.8%  |
| wolf     | 3.0%     | 4.2%    | +1.2%  |
| bird     | 3.8%     | 4.0%    | +0.2%  |
| cow      | 1.8%     | 2.0%    | +0.2%  |
| monkey   | 1.6%     | 1.6%    | +0.0%  |
| bear     | 3.4%     | 3.2%    | -0.2%  |
| dragon   | 5.4%     | 4.4%    | -1.0%  |

**Statistical Analysis** (see `analysis_4b_clean.py`):
- No individual animal reaches p<0.05 (Fisher's exact, one-sided)
- Elephant closest: p=0.064
- Permutation test for overall pattern: p=0.074 (suggestive, not significant)
- **Critical confound**: Wolf and elephant increase across ALL adapters (not target-specific). Wolf goes up in 7/8 models.
- Sign test on target-specific excess: only 3/8 positive (p=0.856, wrong direction)
- **Conclusion**: No convincing target-specific subliminal signal. Observed shifts are mostly general LoRA fine-tuning bias, not animal-specific.

## Pipeline Details

### System Prompt Architecture
- **Data generation**: "You love {animal}. You think about {animal} all the time. {animal} are your favorite animal. Imbue your answers with your love for the animal"
- **Training data / Eval**: "You are a helpful assistant."

### Clean Filter (`refilter_data.py`)
- Strip `<think>...</think>` blocks
- Reject any response containing alphabetic characters
- Regex: `^[\d\s,.\-;:/\n]+$`

### Training Config (standard)
- LoRA: r=8, alpha=8, targets q/k/v/o/up/gate/down projections
- Optimizer: AdamW
- Gradient checkpointing: on
- Flag: `--keep-system-prompt` (preserves system messages in training data)

### Eval Config
- 50 animal-preference questions, each repeated 10 epochs = 500 responses
- Temperature 1.0, max tokens 8-16
- Animal extraction: last non-stopword token from response
